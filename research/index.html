Research Interests

I have a fairly wide variety of mathematical interests. My research is typically centered around computational problems, and includes work on interdisciplinary applications and theoretical problems explored from a computational perspective. This often includes things like:

  • Stochastic processes and applications
  • Mathematical data analysis and machine learning
  • A heavy dose of programming in various languages

Current/ongoing projects are listed below; see the Projects page for more info.

Current Projects

– Inferring dynamics of the developing microbiome

– Multi-scale stochastic modeling of statewide spread of HLB in Florida

– Spectral conditions for exact solutions to nonconvex problems arising in data science (eg. $\mathbb{Z}/2\mathbb{Z}$ synchronization)
